Our Story

ABI Home Inspections Inc. is a home-grown inspection services company founded by Shannon Lewis, a seasoned inspector with over 15 years of experience in the real estate industry. His background in home construction and keen eye for detail ensure that every inspection is thorough and accurate. As a born-and-raised local to the Northern Neck of Virginia, Shannon is committed to serving his community with integrity and transparency.

Our Team

At ABI Home Inspections, we believe that a successful inspection is a collaborative effort. Our team of dedicated professionals works closely with clients, real estate agents, and contractors to ensure that every inspection meets the highest standards. We pride ourselves on our clear communication, detailed reports, and exceptional customer service.
